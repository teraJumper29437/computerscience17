All Saints School

All Saints Catholic School is a private, Catholic school established in 1996 in Oklahoma. It was dedicated to the Archbishop, faculty, administration, staff, parish communities, and strong parental support. The school has grown to 400 students from Norman and surrounding towns in the area. It was awarded a National Blue Ribbon Scholol of Excellence by the U.S. Department of Education. At All Saints Catholic School, they believe children experience growth in an educational tradition firmly rooted in the life and teachings of Jesus. They are a Cahtolic community centered in his Gospel messages of compassion, respect, sacrifice and service to others.
